# Google Ads Cheatsheet Overview

Quick reference for Google Ads campaign types, account structure, and intent-based structuring.

## Campaign Types

| Type | Use Case |
|------|----------|
| Search | High-intent keyword targeting |
| Shopping | E-commerce product ads |
| Display | Awareness and retargeting via banner ads |
| RSA (Responsive Search Ads) | Dynamic headline/description combinations |
| Performance Max | AI-driven across all Google networks |

## Account Structure

Account -> Campaign -> Ad Group

## Account Dimensions

- Network: Google Search, Partner, Display
- Device: Desktop, mobile, tablet
- Location: Geographic targeting
- Audience: In-market, custom, remarketing
- Demographics: Age, gender, income
- Competitors: Brand terms for competitor capture

## Campaign Structuring by Intent

| Campaign Type | Intent Level | Keywords |
|--------------|-------------|----------|
| Brand | Highest | Your brand terms |
| Generic High-Intent | High | Long-tail industry terms |
| Generic Medium/Low Intent | Medium | Short-tail, informational |
| Competitor | Variable | Direct competitor brand KWs |

Budget allocation and ad group structure should follow these intent segments.

## Landing Page Mapping

- Brand -> Homepage
- High-Intent -> Dedicated Landing Page
- Informational -> Content/Comparison Landing Page
- Competitor -> Dedicated Comparison Page
